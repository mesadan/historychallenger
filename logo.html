History Challenger logo concepts

Hourglass-as-C wordmark, plus standalone marks for favicon/social.

A. Wordmark with inline hourglass-C (one line)

The C in "Challenger" is the hourglass. At small sizes it reads as a letterform; at large sizes the hourglass detail comes through.

B. Stacked, dominant mark

The hourglass stands alone as a lockup mark, same height as the two words stacked. Stronger brand presence, a bit more real-estate needed.

C. Horizontal lockup (mark + wordmark)

Works well in a header bar. No special typography trick, just hourglass plus wordmark.

Standalone mark for favicon and social avatar

The mark shrinks cleanly to 16x16 for favicon use.
