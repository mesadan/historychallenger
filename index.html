Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

History Challenger is operated as an independent project. Contact: info@historychallenger.com

What data we collect

Guests: nothing stored on our servers. We use browser local storage to count guest games.

Signed-in users: email address, display name, profile picture URL, game scores and session history, play streak and statistics.

How we use your data

Solely to provide the game experience. We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

Data storage and security

Data is stored in Cloudflare's D1 database infrastructure. We use encrypted connections and secure token-based authentication.

Your rights (GDPR)

EEA users have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data at any time. Contact info@historychallenger.com — we'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use a single authentication token in browser local storage to keep you signed in. No advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

Third-party services

Google OAuth for sign-in and Anthropic's Claude API for event generation.

Children

Not directed at children under 13.
